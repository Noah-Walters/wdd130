Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Stay Grand Vacation Rentals is to provide customers various housing options near the grand canyon. The website will be an easy way to choose between multiple properties and book temporary stays for vacation.

Audience

The audience of the website is for anyone over 21 years old. It will be targeted to those who are looking to visit the Grand Canyon and/or surrounding areas without having to book a hotel.

Content

Home page

Looking for a place to stay that meets all of your needs? Look no more! We have everything you need and more! At Stay Grand Vacation Rentals we offer various housing options to accomodate your travel needs and make your vacation experience that much better. We have many options including houses and cabins ranging from Williams, AZ (the Gateway to the Grand Canyon) all the way to the Grand Canyon National Park!

Images for the Home page

Houses

Whether you're bringing your significant other for a romantic getaway or the whole family for a fun vacation. Catch a break with our diverse list of houses to choose from. We have many options between the historical Williams, AZ and the Grand Canyon National Park. We offer private countryside locations including our very own Luxury Ranch house, the Grand Canyon Wheelhouse, our Romantic Retro Retreat house, and more! We also have locations in town such as the Historic Yellow House, our VIP Luxury Mountain Retreat, and the Casa Del Norte!

Cabins

Here at Stay Grand Vacation Rentals, we also offer cozy and comfy cabin options for the whole family! Some of our more popular cabin properties include: Our Grand Canyon Cabin as well as our Hillside Hideaway. Our cabin properties are perfect for every season and are described as a home away from home.
